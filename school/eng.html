About us

LinguaLab is a modern online English language club in Montenegro for children and adults. Specialists from all around the world make up our team; they are talented and ambitious. Together, we chose engaging programs to help you improve your English as much as possible. LinguaLab offers hundreds of texts on numerous educational subjects, a thorough explanation of the rules, and correcting tasks.

Our team includes specialists with TESOL certificates (Teaching English as a Second Language). The majority of them have expertise working with students from many nations, including Russia, Mongolia, Japan, South Korea, Montenegro, etc.

Since every member of our staff is fluent in Russian, they can smoothly explain grammar concepts and new vocabulary to learners. If you know the language well enough, all interactions will be conducted entirely in English.

We wield a practical interactive platform with videoconferencing to communicate with the club members. You can access your account in the browser without installing any additional software on the device.

Thanks to our online system, you can study in LinguaLab from anywhere in the world. Even while traveling, you can improve your language abilities.

On our platform, we strive to establish the most commodious and comfortable conditions.

Services

For Russian speakers, we have launched nine major group programs:

  • 7–11 years – Beginner and Elementary+ levels;

  • 12–14 years old – Elementary and Pre-intermediate + levels;

  • Intermediate and Upper-intermediate levels for students aged 15 to 18;

  • And Beginner/Elementary, Pre-intermediate/Intermediate, and Upper-intermediate levels are all open for members aged 18.


Classes are held several times a week, and morning and evening groups are available.

Every age and level of proficiency are welcome to the individual Russian-English classes.


With a native English speaker (only):

  • Upper-intermediate level and higher from the age of sixteen.
